# Bioremediation Metadata Guidance

Prioritize remediation context, pollutant identity, site descriptors, treatment conditions, microbial consortia, and environmental measurements.

Common high-value metadata:
- contaminated matrix (soil, water, sediment)
- pollutant or contaminant class
- treatment duration and sampling schedule
- organism or consortium used for remediation
- geographic and environmental descriptors
